## Key Structural Patterns

### The Seven Churches as Church History and End-Time Pairs

- The seven churches represent seven periods of church history (Ephesus → Laodicea).
- They are also **restructured** — Pergamos pairs with Thyatira; Philadelphia pairs with Laodicea.
- The restructuring reveals the end-time church dynamics.

### The Four Horsemen = The First Four Churches

| Horse            | Church   | Color                         |
| ---------------- | -------- | ----------------------------- |
| White Horse      | Ephesus  | White = purity (early church) |
| Red Horse        | Smyrna   | Red = blood (persecution era) |
| Black Horse      | Pergamos | Black = compromise/darkness   |
| Pale Green Horse | Thyatira | Pale green = death/decay      |

### The Trumpets Pattern

- Trumpets 1-4: limited judgments on the natural world.
- Trumpet 5: the first woe — at the center of the 3rd-4th trumpet structure.
- Trumpet 6: the second woe.
- Trumpet 7 (11:15-19): the judgment.
- The 5th and 6th trumpets restructure as one, with the woe at the center.

### Revelation 12-13: The False Trinity

| True Trinity        | False Trinity                                     |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| God the Father      | The Dragon (Satan) — ch. 12                       |
| God the Son         | The Sea Beast (papacy) — ch. 13:1-10              |
| God the Holy Spirit | The Land Beast (false prophet/USA) — ch. 13:11-18 |

### The Three Angels' Messages (14:6-12)

1. **First Angel (vss. 6-7):** "Fear God, and give glory to him; for the hour of his judgment is come: and worship him that made heaven, and earth" — Sabbath, creation, judgment.
2. **Second Angel (vs. 8):** "Babylon is fallen, is fallen" — the fall of apostate Christianity.
3. **Third Angel (vss. 9-12):** "If any man worship the beast and his image, and receive his mark" — the mark of the beast warning, the Sabbath test.
