# Daniel & Revelation Series — Enrichment Plan

## The unifying frame (from the user)

> **Salvation is the restoration of God's image (character).**
> **The study of Revelation is for prophecy to place the day star (Jesus) in our hearts** (2 Pet 1:19).
> **Daniel and Revelation are to reveal the devil's counterfeits.**
> **A verse-by-verse study that builds terminologies and keys so the books can be decoded.**

The 21 studies already have the verse-by-verse + key-building architecture. What this enrichment pass adds is the **theological through-line** that ties prophecy to salvation. Two threads, woven through every study, alongside the seven `[⟲]` threads already in place:

| --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[IMG] Image / character**       | Salvation = restoration of God's image. Every prophetic move is read against: whose image is being formed in whom? **The daystar in 2 Pet 1:19 is Christ formed in the heart — bearing His image _is_ having Him rise in you.** Prophecy's purpose isn't intellectual; it's to make Christ the inner light until the morning breaks. ## What's missing (the gap this plan fills)

Reading the current 21 studies against the user's frame, I see four gaps:

### Gap 1 — The salvation thread is implicit, not explicit

The `What Salvation Really Is` message (`messages/2026-04-24-what-salvation-really-is.md`) compresses the entire arc:

> sin marred the image (Gen 1:26-27 / Gen 5:3) → law is the image in words (Rom 13:10 = 1 John 4:8) → Christ is the image in flesh (Heb 1:3) → sanctification is the image rewritten on the heart (Jer 31:33) → glory = bearing the image (2 Cor 3:18)

This frame **never appears explicitly in the D&R series.** Sanctuary, judgment, mark/seal, millennium — all the prophetic mechanics are there, but the readers never get told that the whole prophetic story is about **whose image is being formed in whom.**

### Gap 2 — Counterfeits are observed but not framed

The series already names individual counterfeits (Sunday vs Sabbath, mark vs seal, image of beast vs Lamb, etc.) but doesn't surface the pattern. **Every counterfeit in Rev 12-16 has a true original earlier in Scripture.** Once the reader sees the pattern — _Satan never invents, only parodies_ — every symbol becomes legible.

### Gap 3 — The remote corpus material isn't yet used

We have ~940 refcodes from the broader EGW corpus (PFF, FAFA, 7SDABC, sermons, letters) that contain **historical receipts** the series currently lacks: the actual record of Vicarius Filii Dei in canon law (PFF2 605, FAFA 226-227), the documentation chain for Litch's 1840 prediction (PFF4 — multiple), the SDA Bible Commentary verse-by-verse — none of which has been pulled into the studies yet.

## The enrichment passes

Four passes, each one a discrete, reviewable batch. Run in order — earlier passes set up what later passes reference.

Christ-in-the-heart (the daystar of 2 Pet 1:19) is the inner side of bearing the image — handled inside [IMG], not as a separate thread.

Estimated effort: passes 1-2 are the largest (real writing); pass 3 is mechanical; pass 4 is half-size. If we batch-commit (one commit per pass), this is 4 sub-commits over a planify-style wave.

## What this plan does NOT do

- **Does not generate new studies.** The 21 studies exist. This is enrichment.
- **Does not violate the "no EGW quotes in studies" rule.** EGW + PFF + DAR are used to _find_ texts and _anchor_ historical claims, not to be quoted in the body.
- **Does not make the series longer for its own sake.** Each insertion must earn its place against the reading-time budget.
- **Does not retrofit the salvation message as a tacked-on appendix.** The [IMG] thread weaves through; it's not a section.
