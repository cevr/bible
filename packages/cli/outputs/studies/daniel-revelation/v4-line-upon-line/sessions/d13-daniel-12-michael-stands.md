# Daniel 12 — Michael Stands

**The same Prince who has _stood for_ Daniel's people now _stands up_ — and the standing
is the last act: the work of mercy done, the kingdom taken, the books closed. Daniel 8
gave the commencement of the final work; Daniel 12 gives the close. And the very delay
between 1843 and the open grave was written into the line in advance — so the
disappointment, when it came, confirmed the doctrine instead of breaking it.**

> The rule of the road: history obeyed the line because the line was drawn first. Watch
> Daniel 12 close the one vision begun in Daniel 8 — and date its own close to the year,
> line upon line.
