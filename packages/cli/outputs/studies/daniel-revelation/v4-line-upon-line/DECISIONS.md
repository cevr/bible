# Line Upon Line — Grilling Decisions Log

Captures every decision made during the `grill-with-docs` interview that produced
this series. If context evaporates, this doc reconstructs exactly what was decided
and why.

**Date of grilling session:** 2026-05-29
**Method:** `grill-with-docs` skill
**Outcome:** a NEW, fourth series under `daniel-revelation/` — `v4-line-upon-line/` —
distinct from v1 (verse-by-verse, archived), v2 (Pioneer Chain, top-level, in-progress),
and v3 (The Sure Word, corrective-for-advanced-readers, in-progress).

**One-breath summary:** A walk through Daniel (1→12) then Revelation (1→22), one
vision per session, where every symbol is decoded by Scripture itself the way
Miller's Rules demand — and as each symbol appears, the session reaches across to its
parallels in the other book and back to earlier visions (`[⟲]`) so the canon's own
recapitulation discloses the truth progressively. Smith's _Daniel and the Revelation_
(DAR) and Haskell's two books (SDP, SSP) are the expositional guides. The time
prophecies (2300, 2520, 1260, 1290, 1335, 6000) are decoded where the text raises
them; the 2520 is taught straight as a valid link Miller forged; the Great
Disappointment is framed as the prophesied tarrying, not a crisis. The series
_demonstrates_ the historicist method by enacting it.

## D0 — v4 is a NEW, separate series (resolved 2026-05-29)

**Question:** The grill produced a positive, foundational, symbol-by-symbol walk. There
is already a partially-drafted v3 "The Sure Word" (its own SERIES-SPEC, DECISIONS,

in its own directory `daniel-revelation/v4-line-upon-line/`, sharing the parent

**Filing note:** during the grill, decisions were initially mis-recorded into the

## D2 — Spine: walk Daniel & Revelation; decode every symbol by Scripture (resolved 2026-05-29)

**Decision:** **The books of Daniel & Revelation ARE the vehicle.** Walk through them;
whenever a symbol appears, **stop and let the Bible define it** (Miller's Rule —
Scripture is its own expositor; a figure is explained by the same Bible elsewhere). A
"Miller's-rules-type study": every symbol decoded by Bible cross-reference, never by
external authority or private meaning.

**How we got here (the grill's path, for the record):** the user first leaned toward a
doctrinal-dependency spine, then "how did Miller build his foundation? — Genesis-forward,"
then "2300 first to prove the year-day," and finally landed on **"Daniel & Revelation
are the vehicle; introduce topics as we come to them — what the symbols mean."** That
final reframe dissolved all the earlier scaffolding (no Genesis run-up, no separate
time-prophecy block, no 2300-as-lens). Progressive disclosure is **automatic** because
Daniel's and Revelation's own recapitulation does it (Dan 2 skeleton → Dan 7 adds
judgment + time → Dan 8 adds 2300 + sanctuary → Dan 9 completes the date → Dan 12 adds
1290/1335 + tarrying). **"Chain by chain" = symbol by symbol; each decoded symbol is a
link; Miller's 14 Rules are the forge.**

## D3 — Keystone: book-rails, cross-references woven via [⟲] (resolved 2026-05-29)

**Question (the deepest of the grill):** "One vision per book in canonical order"
collides with the user's wish to "interleave Daniel & Revelation, disclose by topic,
revisit earlier verses." One must be the spine (rails); the other rides on top.

**Decision:** **Book-rails. Cross-references woven inside each session via `[⟲]`.** The
spine is the canonical walk — **Daniel 1→12, then Revelation 1→22, one vision per
session.** WITHIN a session, when a symbol appears, the session reaches across to the
OTHER book and back to earlier visions to define/enrich it (Dan 7 little horn → pull
Rev 13; the sanctuary → pull Rev 10, Rev 14), each marked `[⟲]`. The reader walks the
books; the weaving happens at each stop.

**Why this resolves the tension rather than splitting it:**

- **Recapitulation IS the progressive disclosure.** Walking Daniel then Revelation, the
  reader meets the beast-power in Dan 7, then re-meets it in Rev 13 _already knowing
  Daniel_ — the second pass is disclosure authored into the canon. No reorder needed.
- **"Go back to older verses" = the `[⟲]` weave** — a within-session technique (the
  marker already exists, parent CONTEXT.md), not a spine reorder.
- **Structure matches receipts** — DAR and Haskell both walk canonically, so every woven
  cross-ref has a citable home in the guides. Topic-rails would orphan sources from
  structure.
- **Draftable and finishable** — every session has an unambiguous home.

The interleave/topic-rails idea is real but lives _inside_ sessions as `[⟲]` weaves,
not as the spine.

## D5 — Session unit = one vision/prophecy (resolved 2026-05-29)

**Decision:** **One complete vision / prophetic unit per session** — NOT one chapter,
NOT a fixed clock-length. Seams follow the text's own visions. A vision too dense for

into its vision. Session count **falls out of the text** (~8-13 for Daniel, ~12-16 for
Revelation; TBD at outline). Chapter divisions are medieval, not inspired — the vision
is the natural unit of disclosure.

## D9 — The sanctuary cluster at the Dan 8 hinge (resolved 2026-05-29)

**Decision:** The sanctuary thread fires three times — TYPE (Lev 16), QUESTION (Dan 8:14
"the sanctuary shall be cleansed"), ANTITYPE (Heb 8-9 + 1844). **Hold the type until
Dan 8:14 forces it.** Do NOT teach Lev 16 in its early canonical position; introduce the
earthly type only when Dan 8:14 raises "which sanctuary, what cleansing?" — so type,
question, and (soon after, across the tarrying) the heavenly antitype cluster as one
dense sanctuary session at the hinge of the Daniel walk.

**Why this doesn't violate book-rails / disclosure:** the type is a **resolution link,
not a foundation link** — only "present truth" once Dan 8:14 raises the question it
answers. Teaching it early would plant the answer before the reader holds the question,
cutting _against_ progressive disclosure. The one deliberate cross-reference-pull is
justified by the disclosure principle itself.

```
Dan 8:14 — 'the sanctuary shall be cleansed' (question fires)
  → the earthly type (Lev 16: courts, daily, Day of Atonement)
  → the question: which sanctuary? what cleansing?
  → [the tarrying — Dan 8:27 'none understood' — D7]
  → Heb 8-9 antitype — the heavenly ministry
  → 1844: Most Holy Place entered, the judgment opened
```
