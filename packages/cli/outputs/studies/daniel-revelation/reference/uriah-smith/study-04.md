### DAR 212.1

> NOTE. — That the expression “to anoint the most holy” refers, according to remarks on verse 24 of this chapter, to the anointing of the heavenly sanctuary previous to the beginning of Christ’s ministry therein, and not to any anointing of the Messiah himself, seems to be susceptible of the clearest proof.
