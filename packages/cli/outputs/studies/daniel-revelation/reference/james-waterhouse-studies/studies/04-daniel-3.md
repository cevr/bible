"Nebuchadnezzar's Golden Statue or Daniel's Three Friends in the Fiery Furnace"

## Key Texts

### The Image: 666 (vs. 1)

- **Daniel 3:1** — "Nebuchadnezzar the king made an image of gold, whose height was **sixty** cubits and whose breadth was **six** cubits."
- Dimensions: 6 x 60 x 6 = **666**
- This is a direct counterfeit of Daniel 2's prophecy — Nebuchadnezzar makes an ALL-GOLD image, rejecting God's revelation that other kingdoms would follow.
- **Revelation 13:18** — "the number of the beast... is Six hundred threescore and six" (666).

### 777 vs. 666

- The 70x7 of Daniel 9 symbolically = the Sabbath number (7th day, 7th year, Jubilee).
- Daniel 3's 60x6 = the counterfeit of 777 = 666.
- **Revelation 13's 666** is the counterfeit of **Creation's 7th-day Sabbath (777)**.
- Daniel 3 (worship the image or die) is the template for Revelation 13 (worship the beast's image or die).

### The Music and Forced Worship (vss. 4-7)

- **vss. 4-5** — "At the time that you hear the sound of the horn, the pipe, the zither/lyre, the sackbut, the psaltery, the bag-pipe, and all kinds of music, you fall down and worship the image."
- **vs. 6** — "He who falls not down and worships... shall be cast into the midst of a burning furnace of fire."
- This is **state-enforced worship** — the template for the mark of the beast (Revelation 13:15).

### The Three Friends' Stand (vss. 16-18)

- **vs. 16** — "We do not need to answer you in this matter."
- **vs. 17** — "If it be so, our God whom we serve is able to deliver us."
- **vs. 18** — "But if not... we will not serve your gods, nor worship the golden image."
- Faith is not contingent on deliverance. They serve God regardless of outcome.

### The Fourth Figure (vs. 25)

- **vs. 25** — "Lo, I see four men loose, walking in the midst of the fire, and they are not hurt; and the form of the fourth is like the Son of God."
- Three were cast in → four are walking. The fourth = Christ.

### Three Friends + Fourth Figure = Four Angels' Messages

| His 3 Friends in the Furnace | 4 Angels' Messages                               |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Hananiah                     | 1st Angel: Worship the Creator (Rev 14:6-7)      |
| Mishael                      | 2nd Angel: Babylon is Fallen (Rev 14:8)          |
| Azariah                      | 3rd Angel: Don't Worship the Beast (Rev 14:9-12) |
| Son of God (the 4th)         | 4th Angel: Come out of Babylon (Rev 18:1-4)      |

### Nebuchadnezzar's Two Decrees

1. **First decree (vss. 4-6):** Worship the image or be killed. — State power enforcing false worship.
2. **Second decree (vss. 29):** Any who speak against the God of Shadrach, Meshach, and Abed-nego shall be destroyed. — The king acknowledges the true God after witnessing deliverance.

### The Furnace Heated Seven-fold (vs. 19)

- "He commanded that they should heat the furnace seven-fold more than it was customary to heat it."
- Seven-fold = complete, total. The persecution will be as intense as possible.

## The Fully Detailed Structure

The full chiastic structure of Daniel 3 is the most complex in the entire book of Daniel, with ~6 nesting levels using Latin letters (A/B/C), Roman numerals (I/II), lowercase letters (a/b), and Hebrew letters (aleph/beth/gimel). The A' section alone (vss. 15b-30) breaks down as:

```
A'  1'  vss. 15b-30
        I    a    i, ii
                  i', ii'
             a'
        II   i    α, β, γ, α', β', γ'         Hebrew: aleph, beth, gimel
             b    i, ii, ii', i'
             b'   i, ii, ii', i'
    1'       i    α, β
             ii
             iii  α    aleph, beth
                  β
                  γ
                  α'   aleph'
                  β'
             iii' γ'   aleph, beth
             ii'       α', β'
             i'
        II        a, a'
    2'
```

Each sub-verse (e.g., vs. 26b-c, 26e-i, 27a-f) has its own position in this hierarchy. This extraordinary detail is itself a finding — God designed this chapter with meticulous literary architecture that cannot be attributed to chance.

## Novel Findings

1. **6x60x6 = 666** — Nebuchadnezzar's image is the first appearance of the number of the beast, 2,600 years before Revelation was written.
2. **Three friends = three angels' messages; fourth figure = fourth angel.** The fiery furnace maps directly to the four angels' messages of Revelation 14 and 18.
3. **The worship test template** — Daniel 3 is the Old Testament template for the mark of the beast scenario: state power commands worship of an image, with death penalty for refusal.
4. **777 vs. 666** — The Sabbath (777) is the counterpart of the image (666). The conflict is always about worship and the day of worship.

## Doctrinal Connections

- **Three Angels' Messages:** The three friends proclaim the three messages by their very stand: worship the Creator (not the image), Babylon's system is fallen (God delivers), don't worship the beast.
- **Sabbath vs. Sunday:** 777 (Sabbath) vs. 666 (counterfeit worship). The final test is over worship.
- **Mark of the Beast:** State-enforced worship with death decree = Daniel 3 = Revelation 13.
- **144,000:** The three friends + the Son of God = four = the 144,000 with Christ.
- **Faith:** "But if not" (v. 18) — faithfulness is not contingent on deliverance.

## Teaching Flow

1. Open with Nebuchadnezzar's response to Daniel 2: he makes an ALL-GOLD image. Why? He rejects the prophecy.
2. The dimensions: 60 cubits x 6 cubits. 6 x 60 x 6 = 666. Connect to Revelation 13:18.
3. The worship command: music plays, everyone bows. State-enforced worship.
4. The three friends refuse. Their confession of faith (vss. 16-18).
5. The furnace: heated seven-fold, three cast in, four walking.
6. The fourth figure: "like the Son of God." Connect to the four angels' messages.
7. 777 vs. 666: the Sabbath is the counter to the beast's mark.
8. Close: this will happen again. Revelation 13 is Daniel 3 repeated. The question is: will you stand?
