# Study 10: Genesis 1-8 — Creation to the Flood: The Great Controversy Pattern

## Sources

- `Daniel and Revelation-structure and text with notes-20240831.pdf` pp. 41-42 (Genesis 1-8 section)

## Theme

"The Fall and the Flood: Creation to Re-Creation" — The entire great controversy pattern encoded in the first eight chapters of the Bible.

## Chiastic Structure

```
A   1   vss. 1:1-2:3     Spirit Hovering Over the Waters (vs. 1:2)
    2                     Appearance of Land (vss. 1:9-10)
    3                     Seventh-day Sabbath (vss. 2:2-3)
B   1   vss. 2:21-25;    Creation of Eve from Side of Adam (vss. 2:21-25)
    2   ch. 3             Eve Sent Out of the Garden (ch. 3)
C       vss. 4:1-16      Conflict Between Cain and Abel
D       vss. 4:17-24     Line of Cain
D'      vss. 4:25-5:32   Line of "Abel" (Seth)
C'      vss. 6:1-7       Sons of God Unite with Daughters of Man
B'  1'  vss. 6:8-10; 7:7 Birth of Noah's Three Sons (vss. 6:8-10)
    2'                    Noah and His Three Sons Enter the Ark (vs. 7:7)
A'  1'  ch. 8            Wind Passes Over the Waters of the Flood (vss. 8:1)
    2'                    Reappearance of Land (vss. 8:1,3,5)
    3'                    Noah Worships God (vs. 8:20)
```

## Key Texts and Prophetic Parallels

### A / A' — Creation and Re-Creation

- **Genesis 1:2** — Spirit hovers over the waters → **Genesis 8:1** — Wind (same Hebrew word as Spirit) passes over the flood waters.
- **Genesis 1:9-10** — Land appears from the waters → **Genesis 8:1,3,5** — Land reappears from the flood.
- **Genesis 2:2-3** — Seventh-day Sabbath → **Genesis 8:20** — Noah worships God.
- The flood is a **re-creation** event. The same pattern: Spirit over waters → land appears → worship.

### B / B' — Eve and Noah's Sons

- **Genesis 2:21-25** — Eve created from Adam's side. → The church created from Christ's side.
- **1 Corinthians 15:45** — "The last Adam was made a quickening spirit."
- **Genesis 2:21** — "The Lord God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept: and he took one of his ribs."
- **John 19:34** — "One of the soldiers with a spear pierced his side, and forthwith came there out blood and water."
- Jesus, the last Adam, from His rib/side gives birth to His wife, the church.
- **Ephesians 5:31-32** — "they two shall be one flesh. This is a great mystery: but I speak concerning Christ and the church."

### C — Conflict Between Cain and Abel (vss. 4:1-16)

- The conflict between truth and error → persecution.
- Culminating in the Protestant Reformation.

### D / D' — Line of Cain and Line of Seth

- **vss. 4:17-24 — Line of Cain:** the Catholic church.
- **vss. 4:25-5:32 — Line of "Abel" (Seth):** the Protestant/Reformed church.

### C' — Sons of God Unite with Daughters of Men (vss. 6:1-7)

- The Protestant/Reformed church unites with the Catholic church.
- **Matthew 24:38-39** — "as in the days of Noe... eating and drinking, **marrying and giving in marriage**" — the union of the two lines.

### B' — Birth of Noah's Three Sons / Entering the Ark

- **vss. 6:8-10 — Birth of Noah's three sons:** the rise of the 144,000, the angels' messages.
- **vs. 7:7 — Noah and his three sons enter the ark:** close of probation. Beginning of the seven plagues, ending with the lake of fire.

### A' — The End of the Flood

- **ch. 8 — The end of the flood:** the recreation of the world after the fire rain and flood of fire or lake of fire.

## Creation Week and the Last Week of Christ

- **vss. 1:1-2:3 — Creation week:** the last week of Christ prior to the crucifixion.
- **vss. 2:21-25 — Creation of Eve from side of Adam:** Jesus, the last Adam, from His rib, gives birth to His wife, the church.

## Eve Sent Out = The Fall of the Church

- **ch. 3 — Eve sent out of the Garden:** the fall of the church.
- **2 Corinthians 11:2-3** — "I have espoused you to one husband, that I may present you as a chaste virgin to Christ. But I fear, lest by any means, as the serpent beguiled Eve through his subtilty, so your minds should be corrupted from the simplicity that is in Christ."

## Novel Findings

1. **Genesis 1-8 encodes the entire great controversy** — Creation, fall, two opposing lines (Cain/Abel = Catholic/Protestant), their union, the rise of the 144,000, close of probation, and re-creation.
2. **The flood is a re-creation event** — same structural pattern as Creation week (Spirit over waters, land appearing, worship).
3. **Eve from Adam's side = the church from Christ's side** — the deep sleep of Adam = the death of Christ; the rib = the spear wound; Eve = the church/bride.
4. **Noah's three sons = the rise of the 144,000** — tied to the three angels' messages.
5. **"Marrying and giving in marriage"** (Matthew 24:38) = the union of the two church lines (Protestant with Catholic), not merely social commentary.

## Doctrinal Connections

- **Sabbath:** Present at Creation (Gen 2:2-3) and at re-creation (Gen 8:20). The Sabbath frames history.
- **144,000:** Noah and his three sons = Daniel and his three friends = the 144,000.
- **Marriage:** Created in Eden as the image of God. Under attack throughout history.
- **Great Controversy:** The entire Cain-Abel-Seth-Flood narrative = the great controversy in miniature.
- **Second Coming / Re-Creation:** The flood pattern is the template for the end: probation closes, judgment falls, earth is re-created.

## Teaching Flow

1. Show the chiastic structure of Genesis 1-8.
2. Creation = re-creation: Spirit over waters, land appears, worship. Same pattern before and after the flood.
3. Eve from Adam's side = the church from Christ's side (John 19:34; Ephesians 5:31-32).
4. Cain and Abel: the first great controversy. Truth vs. error, leading to persecution.
5. Line of Cain (D) vs. line of Seth (D'): the two churches.
6. Their union (C'): "sons of God unite with daughters of men" = ecumenism.
7. Noah and three sons (B'): the 144,000, the angels' messages.
8. The ark: close of probation. The flood: the plagues. The new earth: re-creation.
