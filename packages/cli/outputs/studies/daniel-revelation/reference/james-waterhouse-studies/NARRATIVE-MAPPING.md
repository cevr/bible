| **777 in "weeks seven weeks"**                       | Main PDF pp25-26 | The literal Hebrew of Daniel 9:25 reads "weeks seven weeks" = 7-7-7. **Suggested addition to Part 4:** After the current Daniel 7-9 timeline presentation, add the ram/goat = Christ/Satan layer. The 777 vs. 666 connection makes the numbers personal, not just chronological: the Messiah's prophecy carries the number of perfection, directly opposing the beast's number.

The "cut off" = covenant finding belongs in the Daniel 9 section. When you explain the crucifixion at 31 AD ("in the midst of the week"), show that the Hebrew word for "cut off" is the same word for "cut covenant." Christ was not merely killed. He was the covenant sacrifice. This connects Genesis 15 (Abraham's covenant), Daniel 9 (the Messiah cut off), and Matthew 27 (the veil torn) in a single Hebrew word.
