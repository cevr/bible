# The Garment Spotted By the Flesh

Source: https://sabbathsermons.com/2009/09/01/the-garment-spotted-by-the-flesh/
Fetched: 2026-05-27

## Thesis

Christians, while working among the spiritually degraded, must resist worldly fashion and sensuality — expressed through immodest dress — because clothing reveals character. Apparel that panders to fleshly desires ("spotted by the flesh") must be actively hated and rejected. True godly dress reflects purity, modesty, and neatness, symbolizing Christ's righteousness.

## Key Claims

- Clothing is an index to the heart/character.
- Sheer stockings are immodest garments "spotted by the flesh."
- Exposing an ankle (half-yard of dress lifted) constitutes immodesty.
- Waxing legs is a worldly, fleshly practice introduced with sheer hosiery.
- Tight-lacing/wasp waists are sinful, health-damaging fashion conformity.
- Women wearing men's clothing (trousers) is condemned by Deut. 22:5.
- Aaron's priestly garments are typological models for Christian dress.
- Prevailing social fashion bends character like wind bends trees.
- Pride of appearance = love of self = conformity to worldly lust.
- Style of apparel can "express the truth of the gospel."
