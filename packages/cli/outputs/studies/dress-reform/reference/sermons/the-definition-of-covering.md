# The Definition of Covering

Source: https://sabbathsermons.com/2010/01/14/the-definition-of-covering/
Fetched: 2026-05-27

## Thesis

Modest dress means full physical covering at all times and in all movements — for both men and women — reflecting spiritual submission to God's order, including head covering (long hair), clothed limbs, modest shoes, and natural-fiber garments.

## Key Claims

- Clothing must cover fully during every movement — bending, sitting, reaching.
- Hemline/style need not change seasonally; only fabric weight changes.
- Pants under dress protect from "sensual feelings" and prevent health issues in later years.
- Shoes should be wide-toed, low-heeled; sandals are historically linked to sensuality.
- Long sleeves are spiritually and physically beneficial year-round.
- Children should be dressed by the same standards as adults.
- Garments must be single-fiber, natural fabric (per Lev. 19:19).
- A woman's long hair is her divinely ordained covering, signifying submission to God's order.
- Angels observe and respond to whether women display this sign of submission.
- Women's limbs require as much clothing as men's — only 1 in 1,000 women clothe their limbs adequately.
- Character is revealed by dress; "The style of dress is the index of the heart."
- Jesus wore shoes, not sandals (Matthew 3:11 cited).
