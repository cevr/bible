# Sabbath Sermons — Dress Reform Sermon Index

Fetched: 2026-05-27
Source domain: sabbathsermons.com (all sermons attributed to John Thiel unless noted)

## Sermons

1. **a-spectacle-for-the-one-i-love** — Faithful Christians become a "spectacle" to a satanically-conditioned world; singular dress and modesty are marks of loving Jesus.
2. **7-gods-covering-glory-dress-reform** — God's glory is His character; dress visibly reflects that character. Detailed standards on hemline, limbs, colors, gender distinction, fabric.
3. **the-definition-of-covering** — Modest dress = full physical covering in every movement; long hair = head covering; angels respond to women's sign of submission.
4. **18-gods-way-of-dress-part-2** — Priestly garments establish the timeless standard; dress reform = the "ribbon of blue" for the remnant; rejects American costume and floor-sweeping fashion.
5. **19-gods-way-of-dress-part-3** — Fiber matters: no mingled fabrics. The "Babylonish garment" of Achan ties dress to spiritual purity. Style and fiber both connect to holiness.
6. **the-garment-spotted-by-the-flesh** — Believers must actively _hate_ worldly fashion; clothing indexes the heart; sheer stockings, wasp waists, and ankle-exposure named as spotted garments.
7. **the-fashions-of-babylon** — OT fringes/ribbon of blue contrasted with Babylonian fashion; Achan/Jacob narratives ground the warning. (Scripture-only; no EGW citations.)
8. **the-beauty-of-holiness** — True beauty is spiritually discerned; only the born-again can perceive holiness as beautiful; priestly garments are the type.
9. **dress-reform-and-the-sanctuary-part-1** — Sanctuary typology: body = courtyard, mind = first apartment, frontal lobe = Most Holy Place. Light-covering of Eden, coats of skins, priestly garments form one chain.
10. **adornment-for-the-one-we-love** — Redemptive love transforms the inner spirit, which then manifests as modest dress; ponytails as proud-horse imagery; "any part displayed is contrary to the cross of Christ."

## Recurring Theological Claims (Deduplicated)

### A. Dress as Index of Character / Heart

_Sermons:_ a-spectacle; 7-gods-covering-glory; definition-of-covering; 18-gods-way-part-2; garment-spotted; beauty-of-holiness; adornment.

- Outward dress reflects inward character. EGW: "Dress is an index of the mind and heart." "The style of dress is the index of the heart."

### B. Adam & Eve Were Clothed in Light Before the Fall

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; sanctuary-part-1; adornment.

- Eden nakedness ≠ exposed flesh. Sin removed the robe of light; coats of skins replaced it.

### C. Aaron's Priestly Garments as Typological Standard

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; definition-of-covering; 18-gods-way-part-2; 19-gods-way-part-3; garment-spotted; beauty-of-holiness; sanctuary-part-1; adornment.

- "For glory and for beauty" (Ex 28:2) — full body covering, dense linen, no form revealed.

### D. Gender Distinction (Deut 22:5) Forbids Women in Men's Clothing

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; 18-gods-way-part-2; garment-spotted; fashions-of-babylon.

- "American costume"/pantsuits = abomination to God.

### E. Limbs Must Be Covered as Thoroughly as Men's

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; definition-of-covering; 18-gods-way-part-2; garment-spotted; adornment.

- "But one woman in a thousand who clothes her limbs as she should." Pants under dress; tapered ankle.

### F. Hemline Standard: Below the Top of the Boot, Clearing Street Filth

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; 18-gods-way-part-2; garment-spotted (re: half-yard lift).

- The three companies vision: floor-sweeping = too long; above-knee = too short; "a few inches" clearance = correct.

### G. No Mingled Fibers (Lev 19:19; Deut 22:11)

_Sermons:_ definition-of-covering; 19-gods-way-part-3; fashions-of-babylon.

- Pure single-fiber natural fabric = type of Christ's unmixed righteousness.

### H. The "Babylonish Garment" / Babylonian Fashion as Spiritual Defilement

_Sermons:_ 19-gods-way-part-3; the-fashions-of-babylon; the-beauty-of-holiness.

- Achan's Babylonish garment was the accursed thing. Following fashion = copying Babylon.

### I. Long Hair as Woman's Divine Head Covering / Angelic Sign

_Sermons:_ definition-of-covering; sanctuary-part-1; adornment (comments).

- 1 Cor 11 head covering = long hair; angels minister to those with the sign of submission.

### J. Dress Reform = the Modern Ribbon of Blue (Num 15)

_Sermons:_ 18-gods-way-part-2; 19-gods-way-part-3; fashions-of-babylon.

- A visible identity marker for the remnant, paralleling Israel's fringe.

### K. Worldly Fashion is a Satanic / Demonic Conditioning Progression

_Sermons:_ a-spectacle; 7-gods-covering-glory; garment-spotted.

- Stages: ankle exposure → sheer stockings → shorts → swimwear → trousers on women → end-of-world marker.

### L. Christians Will Be a "Spectacle" — Singular, Mocked, Peculiar

_Sermons:_ a-spectacle; 7-gods-covering-glory (peculiar); 18-gods-way-part-2 (peculiar people).

- 1 Cor 4:9; willing to be ridiculous in the world's eyes.

### M. Health/Dress Reform Conjoined (Corsets, Tight-Lacing, Heavy Skirts)

_Sermons:_ 18-gods-way-part-2; garment-spotted.

- Female form must not be compressed; hips not designed to bear heavy weights.

### N. Modesty = No Form-Revealing / Transparent / Tight Clothing

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; 18-gods-way-part-2; sanctuary-part-1; adornment.

- "Thoroughly cloaked"; "any part displayed is contrary to the cross of Christ."

### O. New Converts Must Be Specifically Instructed on Dress at Baptism

_Sermons:_ beauty-of-holiness; sanctuary-part-1 (via 6T 96.1).

- "The idolatry of dress is a moral disease. It must not be taken over into the new life."

### P. Daughters of Zion (Isa 3) Applies to Last-Days Church Women

_Sermons:_ 19-gods-way-part-3; garment-spotted; fashions-of-babylon; sanctuary-part-1; adornment.

- Haughtiness, mincing walk, ornaments — all to be removed in judgment.

### Q. Style of Apparel Will Express the Truth of the Gospel

_Sermons:_ 7-gods-covering-glory; 19-gods-way-part-3; garment-spotted; adornment.

### R. The True Christian Adornment is the Meek and Quiet Spirit (1 Pet 3:3–4)

_Sermons:_ beauty-of-holiness; adornment.

- Inner ornament displaces external display.

### S. Sandals Are Linked Historically with Sensuality

_Sermons:_ definition-of-covering.

### T. Jacob's Household Putting Away Ornaments (Gen 35) as Dress-Reform Precedent

_Sermons:_ 19-gods-way-part-3; fashions-of-babylon.
