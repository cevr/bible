# Study 13: The Final Crisis

**The judgment is underway. Babylon is exposed. God's people have been called out. Now comes the test. Every truth restored since 1844 converges into a single question of allegiance: whose seal will you bear? The crisis is not about a day — it is about authority. And the battle is not against flesh and blood — it is for the mind.**

## Prep Reading

- Revelation 13:11-17
- Revelation 18:1-4
- Revelation 14:9-12
- Joel 2:23, 28-29
- Daniel 3:1-18
- Ephesians 6:11-17

## The Latter Rain — God's Final Empowerment

- **The early rain planted; the latter rain ripens**
  - "Be glad then, ye children of Zion, and rejoice in the LORD your God: for he hath given you the former rain moderately, and he will cause to come down for you the rain, the former rain, and the latter rain" (Joel 2:23)
  - The early rain fell at Pentecost — the Spirit empowered the apostles to launch the gospel to the world
  - The latter rain falls at the end — the Spirit empowers God's people to give the final message
  - "And it shall come to pass afterward, that I will pour out my spirit upon all flesh; and your sons and your daughters shall prophesy" (Joel 2:28)
  - [→] Pentecost was the early rain. The latter rain is the antitype — the same Spirit, the same power, but for the final harvest.

- **Ask for the rain**
  - "Ask ye of the LORD rain in the time of the latter rain; so the LORD shall make bright clouds, and give them showers of rain" (Zech 10:1)
  - The latter rain is not automatic. It must be sought. It comes to those who are preparing for it.
  - [→] The early rain prepared the disciples for Pentecost. The latter rain prepares God's people for the final crisis. But the ground must be ready — a heart hardened by sin cannot absorb the rain.

- **The husbandman waits**
  - "Be patient therefore, brethren, unto the coming of the Lord. Behold, the husbandman waiteth for the precious fruit of the earth, and hath long patience for it, until he receive the early and latter rain" (James 5:7)
  - God is waiting for the harvest to ripen. The latter rain ripens it.
  - [→] Study 9 said God is waiting for characters to be transformed. The latter rain is the power that completes the transformation. No latter rain, no harvest. No harvest, no Second Coming.

## The Loud Cry — The Earth Lightened

- **Revelation 18:1 — the fourth angel**
  - "And after these things I saw another angel come down from heaven, having great power; and the earth was lightened with his glory" (Rev 18:1)
  - This angel joins the three of Revelation 14. The message is the same — but now with overwhelming power.
  - "The earth was lightened with his glory" — glory = character (Ex 33-34, Study 3). The earth is lightened by the character of God reflected in His people.
  - [→] The loud cry is not just a louder sermon. It is the Three Angels' Messages amplified by the lives of those who give them. The message and the messenger become one.

- **The gospel to all the world — then the end**
  - "And this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world for a witness unto all nations; and then shall the end come" (Matt 24:14)
  - The loud cry completes this commission. Every nation, kindred, tongue, and people will hear.
  - [→] The loud cry is the last call. After it, every person has chosen. The harvest is ripe.

- **"Come out of her, my people" — the final call**
  - "And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues" (Rev 18:4)
  - Study 9 gave this call. The loud cry intensifies it. People stream out of Babylon in response to truth presented with the power of the Spirit.
  - [→] The loud cry produces a great separation. Those who respond come out. Those who refuse harden. The lines are drawn.

## The Image of the Beast — Church and State Unite

- **Revelation 13:11 — the second beast**
  - "And I beheld another beast coming up out of the earth; and he had two horns like a lamb, and he spake as a dragon" (Rev 13:11)
  - Lamb-like horns = Christian profession, republican principles
  - Dragon voice = the spirit of coercion, force, persecution
  - [→] A power that looks Christian but speaks like Satan. A power that professes liberty but enforces worship.

- **It makes an image to the first beast**
  - "Saying to them that dwell on the earth, that they should make an image to the beast, which had the wound by a sword, and did live" (Rev 13:14)
  - The first beast = the papal system (Studies 5-7). An image to the beast = a replica of its method: church-state union enforcing religious observance.
  - "And he had power to give life unto the image of the beast, that the image of the beast should both speak, and cause that as many as would not worship the image of the beast should be killed" (Rev 13:15)
  - [→] What the papacy did in the Dark Ages, the image of the beast reproduces at the end: religious law enforced by civil power. The method is the same. The target is the same.

- **Daniel 3 — the prototype**
  - "Nebuchadnezzar the king made an image of gold... and whoso falleth not down and worshippeth shall the same hour be cast into the midst of a burning fiery furnace" (Dan 3:1, 6)
  - State-mandated worship. A single standard imposed on all. Death for noncompliance.
  - Three Hebrews refused: "We are not careful to answer thee in this matter. If it be so, our God whom we serve is able to deliver us... But if not, be it known unto thee, O king, that we will not serve thy gods, nor worship the golden image which thou hast set up" (Dan 3:16-18)
  - [→] "But if not" — this is the faith of Jesus. Obedience without guaranteed deliverance. The same faith Job expressed (Job 13:15). The same faith the 144,000 will express. Daniel 3 is the rehearsal.

[ILL] Nebuchadnezzar's image was 60 cubits by 6 cubits — 6 and 6. The music played, the nations bowed, and three men stood. The entire book of Revelation is Daniel 3 at global scale. One image. One command. One test. And a remnant who will not bow.

## The Mark of the Beast vs. the Seal of God

- **The mark — enforced counterfeit worship**
  - "And he causeth all, both small and great, rich and poor, free and bond, to receive a mark in their right hand, or in their foreheads" (Rev 13:16)
  - "And that no man might buy or sell, save he that had the mark" (Rev 13:17)
  - Forehead = conviction — those who genuinely believe the counterfeit
  - Hand = compliance — those who go along to survive
  - Economic boycott: no buying, no selling. Social death before physical death.

- **What is the mark?**
  - Study 10 established: the seal of God is in the fourth commandment — name, title, territory (Ex 20:8-11)
  - The mark of the beast is the counterfeit of the seal — a rival claim to authority
  - The counterfeit system claimed power to change the day of worship from Sabbath to Sunday (Dan 7:25)
  - The mark is not merely Sunday observance — it is the knowing, deliberate choice to follow human authority over divine authority after the truth has been made plain
  - [→] No one receives the mark today. The mark is received when the issue is made clear, the choice is forced, and a person chooses the counterfeit with full knowledge. Until that crisis arrives, Sunday-keepers who love God sincerely are His people — that is why He calls them out (Rev 18:4).

- **The seal — God's mark of ownership and character**
  - "Hurt not the earth, neither the sea, nor the trees, till we have sealed the servants of our God in their foreheads" (Rev 7:3)
  - The seal is placed before the crisis reaches its climax. God's people are secured before the storm.
  - Study 10: the Sabbath is the sign of creation, redemption, sanctification, and completion. It is the seal because it carries God's authority and marks His finished work in the character.
  - [→] Mark vs. seal. Two authorities. Two signs. Two allegiances. The crisis makes the invisible visible — every heart's true loyalty is revealed.

[Q] **"Is the mark just about Saturday vs. Sunday?"**

> It is never just about a day. It is about what the day represents. The Sabbath carries the Creator's seal — His authority, His law, His character. Sunday, when enforced as a substitute, carries the authority of the system that changed it. The final crisis forces the question: whose authority do you recognize? The Creator's or the counterfeit's? The day is the test; the authority is the issue.

## The Battle for the Mind — Deception, Not Force Alone

- **False Christs and lying wonders**
  - "For there shall arise false Christs, and false prophets, and shall shew great signs and wonders; insomuch that, if it were possible, they shall deceive the very elect" (Matt 24:24-25)
  - "Even him, whose coming is after the working of Satan with all power and signs and lying wonders, and with all deceivableness of unrighteousness" (2 Thess 2:9-10)
  - The crisis is not only economic boycott and death threats. It is deception — miracles that look divine but originate from below.
  - [→] Satan does not rely on force alone. He never has. From Eden onward, his primary weapon is deception. The final crisis combines coercion with counterfeit miracles. Both senses and survival are under attack.

- **Satan as angel of light**
  - "And no marvel; for Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light" (2 Cor 11:14)
  - The final deception will not look evil. It will look good, feel right, and appear spiritual.
  - [→] This is why the mind must be clear (Study 11), the body must be cared for (Study 11), and the Word must be the sole authority (Isa 8:20). When miracles contradict Scripture, Scripture wins. Every time.

- **Spirits of devils working miracles**
  - "For they are the spirits of devils, working miracles, which go forth unto the kings of the earth and of the whole world, to gather them to the battle of that great day of God Almighty" (Rev 16:13-14)
  - Three unclean spirits — from the dragon (paganism/spiritualism), the beast (papal apostasy), and the false prophet (apostate Protestantism)
  - A global coalition united by supernatural deception
  - [→] The entire world is gathered. The pressure is universal. The only defense is the Word of God and the faith of Jesus.

- **The only defense: the Word**
  - "To the law and to the testimony: if they speak not according to this word, it is because there is no light in them" (Isa 8:20)
  - "Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil" (Eph 6:11)
  - "And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God" (Eph 6:17)
  - Christ defeated Satan in the wilderness with "It is written" — three times. The same weapon is the only one that works at the end.
  - [→] The battle for the mind is won or lost before the crisis arrives. The time to fill the mind with the Word is now — not when the test comes. Daniel purposed in his heart _before_ the test (Dan 1:8). The three Hebrews knew their answer _before_ the furnace was heated (Dan 3:16-18).

## The Sequence — How It Unfolds

The crisis is not a single event but a sequence:

1. **Latter rain falls** — the Spirit empowers God's people for the final work (Joel 2:28-29)
2. **Loud cry goes forth** — the Three Angels' Messages given with overwhelming power; the earth is lightened with God's glory (Rev 18:1)
3. **Separation accelerates** — God's people come out of Babylon; the lines harden (Rev 18:4)
4. **Sunday law enacted** — civil power enforces religious observance; the image of the beast speaks (Rev 13:15)
5. **Economic boycott** — no buying or selling without the mark (Rev 13:17)
6. **Death decree** — the final escalation; those who refuse to worship are condemned to death (Rev 13:15)
7. **Miraculous deceptions** — lying wonders, false Christs, spirits of devils unite the world against God's remnant (Matt 24:24; Rev 16:13-14)
8. **Sealing completed** — God's people are sealed; character work is finished (Rev 7:1-3)
9. **Close of probation** — "He that is unjust, let him be unjust still: and he that is righteous, let him be righteous still" (Rev 22:11)

[→] After probation closes, no more intercession. No more opportunity to change sides. The cases are decided. What follows is the time of trouble — Study 14.

## Rev 14:12 — The Answer Stands

- "Here is the patience of the saints: here are they that keep the commandments of God, and the faith of Jesus" (Rev 14:12)
- In the middle of the worst crisis earth has ever seen, a people endure.
- They keep the commandments — including the fourth. Charge B answered.
- They have the faith of Jesus — trust without visible reward, loyalty unto death. Charge A answered.
- [→] The entire series has been building to this verse. Study 1 filed the charges. Studies 2-12 traced the war. Study 13 describes the final battle. And Rev 14:12 is the verdict that the battle produces.
