# Study 10: Come Out of Her, My People

**The counterfeit is exposed. The judgment is underway. Now the demanded response: God's people must leave Babylon. But "come out" is not merely changing organizations — it is leaving the accuser's jurisdiction for the Judge's courtroom. Babylon does not teach random errors. It teaches Satan's charges as doctrine. To come out is to abandon the theology of the accuser and embrace the truth that answers him.**

## Prep Reading

- Revelation 18:1-5
- Isaiah 58:12-14
- 1 Timothy 2:5
- Acts 17:10-11
- 2 Peter 3:9

---

## God's People Are Still Inside

- **Revelation 18:4 — the call**
  - "And I heard another voice from heaven, saying, Come out of her, my people, that ye be not partakers of her sins, and that ye receive not of her plagues" (Rev 18:4)
  - Two words change everything: "my people"
  - These are not pagans. Not atheists. Not the indifferent.
  - These are sincere believers — people who love God — trapped inside a system that has lied to them

- **God loves them — that is why He calls**
  - The call is not condemnation. It is rescue.
  - A judge does not warn people he intends to destroy without notice
  - "As many as I love, I rebuke and chasten: be zealous therefore, and repent" (Rev 3:19)
  - [→] If God had no people inside Babylon, He would not need to call anyone out. The urgency of the call proves the sincerity of those still within.

- **"Come out" — not "stay and reform"**
  - The call is not to fix Babylon. It is to leave it.
  - "Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen, and is become the habitation of devils, and the hold of every foul spirit" (Rev 18:2)
  - You do not renovate a condemned building. You evacuate it.
  - [→] Reform implies the system can be corrected. God says it has become the habitation of devils. You cannot sanctify a structure designed for deception.

[Q] **"But there are good people in every church. Why can't they just stay where they are?"**

> Good people are exactly who God is addressing. He is not calling bad people out — He is calling HIS people out. Sincerity does not sanctify error. A sincere believer drinking Babylon's wine is still intoxicated. Love demands the call, not silence.

- **what "coming out" looks like practically**
  - testing every belief against Scripture — "These were more noble than those in Thessalonica, in that they received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily, whether those things were so" (Acts 17:11)
  - being willing to follow truth even when it costs — reputation, relationships, comfort. Loyalty to light received is the price of more light.
  - recognizing that coming out is a process, not a single moment — God leads step by step, and each step of obedience opens the next. The call is ongoing.

## Babylon Teaches Satan's Charges as Doctrine

- **Babylon = confusion**
  - "Therefore is the name of it called Babel; because the LORD did there confound the language of all the earth" (Gen 11:9)
  - Not random confusion — strategic confusion. Truth mixed with error until neither is recognizable.
  - "For such are false apostles, deceitful workers, transforming themselves into the apostles of Christ. And no marvel; for Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light" (2 Cor 11:13-14)

- **the counterfeits are not arbitrary — they are Satan's accusations institutionalized**
  - Study 1 established Satan's two charges against God in the cosmic trial
  - Those charges did not stay in heaven. They became the theology of Babylon.
  - [→] Every false doctrine Babylon teaches can be traced back to one of Satan's original accusations. The counterfeit system is the accuser's courtroom brief turned into a creed.

### Charge #1 Institutionalized: "They Only Serve God for Reward"

- **Satan's original charge**
  - "Doth Job fear God for nought? Hast not thou made an hedge about him?" (Job 1:9-10)
  - Loyalty is transactional. Remove the benefits and obedience collapses.
  - No one serves God from love — only from self-interest

- **Babylon teaches this as gospel**
  - prosperity gospel — serve God and He will make you rich, healthy, successful
  - cheap grace — believe the right formula, receive the reward, behavior irrelevant
  - religion as transaction: give tithes, get blessings; say the prayer, get the ticket
  - the cross becomes a bargain counter instead of a place of death to self
  - [→] When the church teaches that following God is the path to personal advantage, it is preaching Satan's charge as doctrine. It is confirming his accusation: they serve You for what they get.

- **Job's friends taught the same theology**
  - "Shall mortal man be more just than God? shall a man be more pure than his maker?" (Job 4:17)
  - "What is man, that he should be clean? and he which is born of a woman, that he should be righteous?" (Job 15:14)
  - "How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman?" (Job 25:4)
  - Job's friends sound devout. They quoted theology. But their theology was Satan's — that God's people are inherently incapable of genuine loyalty
  - [→] Religious language does not make theology true. Job's friends used pious words to deliver the accuser's argument.

### Charge #2 Institutionalized: "The Law Cannot Be Kept"

- **Satan's original charge**
  - God demands too much. His law is impossible. Obedience under fallen conditions is a fiction.
  - If this is true, God is either a tyrant (demanding the impossible) or a fraud (pretending His law matters while ignoring violations)

- **Babylon teaches this as humility**
  - "no one is perfect" — stated not as a confession but as a theological ceiling
  - "we sin every day in thought, word, and deed" — recited as creed, not lamented as failure
  - character transformation dismissed as "perfectionism"
  - obedience dismissed as "legalism"
  - Sunday replaces Sabbath — the law itself is considered changeable, disposable, obsolete
  - [→] When a church teaches that God's law cannot be kept, it is not being humble. It is agreeing with Satan's accusation and calling it orthodoxy.

- **but Scripture says otherwise**
  - "But we all, with open face beholding as in a glass the glory of the Lord, are changed into the same image from glory to glory, even as by the Spirit of the Lord" (2 Cor 3:18)
  - "Be ye therefore perfect, even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect" (Matt 5:48)
  - "I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me" (Phil 4:13)
  - Not human achievement — divine transformation. Changed by beholding, not by striving.
  - [→] Christ does not command what He does not enable. "Be ye perfect" is not mockery — it is a promise wearing the grammar of a command.

[DYK] The word translated "perfect" in Matthew 5:48 is teleios — meaning complete, mature, reaching the intended goal. It is not a demand for sinless performance by human effort. It is a declaration that God intends to bring His people to full maturity of character. Babylon says He can't. Christ says He will.

## What They Must Leave — The Accuser's Theology Made Doctrine

- **Revelation 18:2-3 — what Babylon has become**
  - "Babylon the great is fallen, is fallen, and is become the habitation of devils, and the hold of every foul spirit, and a cage of every unclean and hateful bird" (Rev 18:2)
  - "For all nations have drunk of the wine of the wrath of her fornication, and the kings of the earth have committed fornication with her" (Rev 18:3)
  - Doctrines of demons (1 Tim 4:1) dressed as Christian teaching
  - Church-state union — "the kings of the earth have committed fornication with her" (Rev 17:2) — spiritual authority enforced by political power

- **Sunday replacing Sabbath — denies God's authority**
  - The Sabbath carries the seal of the Creator: who He is, what He did, what is His (Ex 20:8-11)
  - Replace the day and you replace the authority behind it
  - "Think to change times and laws" (Dan 7:25) — the little horn's signature act
  - [→] The Sabbath is not a preference. It is the Creator's claim on time itself. Replacing it is not a scheduling decision — it is a jurisdictional challenge.

- **immortal soul replacing resurrection — denies need for judgment**
  - if the dead are already in heaven or hell, judgment is irrelevant — the verdict was already executed at death
  - purgatory, prayers for the dead, saint intercession — all built on the lie that the dead are conscious
  - "The dead know not any thing" (Eccl 9:5)
  - [→] The immortal soul doctrine does not merely add an error. It removes the entire framework of judgment, resurrection, and the Second Coming. One lie collapses three truths.

- **confessional replacing Christ's mediation — denies His priestly work**
  - a human priest hearing confession and pronouncing absolution
  - "Who can forgive sins but God only?" (Mark 2:7)
  - "For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus" (1 Tim 2:5)
  - [→] Every human mediator standing between the sinner and Christ is a re-enactment of Babylon's original counterfeit: Nimrod as priest-king, controlling access to God.

- **each counterfeit reinforces Satan's case**
  - Sunday says: the law can be changed — supporting the charge that it was arbitrary to begin with
  - immortal soul says: there is no real death and no real judgment — eliminating accountability
  - human priesthood says: you cannot approach God directly — denying the access Christ secured
  - cheap grace says: behavior does not matter — confirming that transformation is impossible
  - [→] Strip away the religious vocabulary and Babylon's doctrines are Satan's closing argument: God's law is unreasonable, His judgment is unnecessary, and His people cannot be transformed.

[ILL] A prisoner of war is fed propaganda by his captors until he begins to repeat it as his own belief. He is sincere. He is convinced. He is still wrong. And the first step of rescue is not an argument — it is extraction. "Come out of her, my people" is a rescue operation for prisoners who have internalized the captor's worldview.

## What They Must Embrace — Truth That Answers the Charges

- **every truth God restores directly dismantles a specific charge**
  - this is not coincidence — it is counter-argument
  - God does not merely correct errors. He rebuilds the case that the errors were designed to destroy.

- **one Mediator, not human priests**
  - "For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus" (1 Tim 2:5)
  - direct access to God through Christ alone — no gatekeeper, no intermediary, no toll booth
  - truth restored: the sanctuary has a High Priest, and He is not on earth

- **Sabbath rest, not Sunday**
  - "There remaineth therefore a rest to the people of God" (Heb 4:9)
  - the Greek sabbatismos — a Sabbath-keeping — is the word used
  - the Creator's sign, embedded in creation, confirmed at Sinai, kept by Christ, binding still
  - truth restored: God's law is not obsolete, and the seal in its center has not been moved

- **death as sleep, not immortal soul**
  - "These things said he: and after that he saith unto them, Our friend Lazarus sleepeth; but I go, that I may awake him out of sleep... Then said Jesus unto them plainly, Lazarus is dead" (John 11:11, 14)
  - the dead await resurrection — they do not pass immediately to reward or punishment
  - truth restored: the resurrection and the judgment are necessary, real, and coming

- **judgment by law, not cheap grace**
  - "So speak ye, and so do, as they that shall be judged by the law of liberty" (James 2:12)
  - grace does not abolish the law — grace empowers obedience to it
  - the law is the standard of judgment, and the judgment is real
  - truth restored: God's moral government stands, and His people can and will be measured by it

- **Second Coming hope**
  - "Looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ" (Titus 2:13)
  - not death as the gateway to eternity — but Christ's personal, visible, bodily return
  - truth restored: history is going somewhere, and the destination is not a disembodied heaven but a restored earth

[→] Each restored truth is a direct rebuttal to one of Babylon's counterfeits. Together they form God's complete answer to every charge Satan has institutionalized.

- **Isaiah 58:12 — the mission of God's last-day people**
  - "And they that shall be of thee shall build the old waste places: thou shalt raise up the foundations of many generations; and thou shalt be called, The repairer of the breach, the restorer of paths to dwell in" (Isa 58:12)
  - the "breach" = what was broken when truth was cast to the ground (Dan 8:12)
  - the "paths to dwell in" = the original truths — Sabbath, sanctuary, state of the dead, salvation by faith
  - [→] "Repairer of the breach" is not a metaphor. It is a job description. God's people are called to rebuild exactly what the counterfeit system demolished.

## Why the Delay? Coming Out Is Necessary but Not Enough

- **God is patient — not indecisive**
  - "The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, as some men count slackness; but is longsuffering to us-ward, not willing that any should perish, but that all should come to repentance" (2 Pet 3:9)
  - The call has been going out since 1844. Why hasn't it ended?
  - Because God is waiting for His people to respond — not merely with organizational departure but with character transformation

- **the rebuke to Satan is not yet complete**
  - leaving Babylon answers the question of allegiance — but not the question of character
  - Satan's deepest charge is not that God's people are in the wrong institution — it is that they cannot be transformed
  - coming out of Babylon is necessary but insufficient
  - [→] You can leave Babylon on Saturday morning and bring Babylon's theology with you in your heart. Geography is not transformation.

- **the refiner's fire**
  - "But who may abide the day of his coming? and who shall stand when he appeareth? for he is like a refiner's fire, and like fullers' soap: and he shall sit as a refiner and purifier of silver, and he shall purify the sons of Levi" (Mal 3:2-3)
  - a refiner watches silver until he can see his own reflection in it
  - God watches His people until His character is reflected in theirs
  - this is not punishment — it is completion

- **the husbandman waits for fruit**
  - "Be patient therefore, brethren, unto the coming of the Lord. Behold, the husbandman waiteth for the precious fruit of the earth, and hath long patience for it, until he receive the early and latter rain" (James 5:7)
  - God is not waiting for a date on a calendar. He is waiting for a harvest.
  - the precious fruit = character transformed by truth, not merely informed by it
  - [→] The delay is not God's reluctance. It is His patience. He is waiting for His people to become what the truth makes possible — living answers to every charge the accuser has filed.

[TANGENT] This is why merely accepting correct doctrine is not enough. Satan knows every true doctrine. He could pass any theology exam. The difference between a demon who knows truth and a saint who lives it is not information — it is transformation. Babylon's people must not only learn the truth but be changed by it.

## Appeal

- God has people inside Babylon right now — sincere, devout, and deceived
- He is calling them out — not in anger, but in love (Rev 18:4)
- What they must leave is not merely an institution — it is a theology. Satan's charges dressed as Christian doctrine.
- What they must embrace is not merely a set of corrections — it is truth that answers the accuser point by point
- Sabbath, sanctuary, state of the dead, salvation by faith, the Second Coming — each one dismantles a specific lie
- "Thou shalt be called, The repairer of the breach, the restorer of paths to dwell in" (Isa 58:12)
- But coming out is the beginning, not the end
- God is waiting — not for more information to circulate, but for characters to be transformed
- He is the refiner watching silver. He is the husbandman waiting for fruit.
- The question is not just "Will you leave Babylon?" but "Will you let truth do its full work in you?"
